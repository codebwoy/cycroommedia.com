# Trust-Building Content Checklist

Update these placeholders to make trust undeniable on your site.

## 1. Live Counter
- **Location:** Hero section
- **Update:** Change `data-count="7"` and the visible number in `index.html` (line ~45) as you add clients
- **Tip:** Keep it accurate — overstating hurts trust

## 2. About Section
- **Location:** "Meet the Team" section
- **Add:** Your photo (replace the placeholder div with `<img src="your-photo.jpg" alt="Your Name">`)
- **Write:** 3 sentences: why you started, what drives you, what makes you different
- **Tip:** People buy from people. A real face doubles trust.

## 3. Before/After Screenshots
- **Location:** "The Transformation" section
- **Before:** Screenshot of a shop's messy WhatsApp order chat
- **After:** Screenshot of their clean new Cycroom app
- **How:** Replace the `.image-placeholder` divs with `<img src="before.jpg">` and `<img src="after.jpg">`
- **Tip:** This is the single most powerful image on the site

## 4. Video Testimonials
- **Location:** "What Shop Owners Say" section
- **Add:** 30-second phone videos from real shop owners saying "this changed my business"
- **Tip:** Offer a free month to your first 3 clients in exchange for a short video
- **How:** Use `<video>` or embed YouTube/Vimeo in each placeholder
